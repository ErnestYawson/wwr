History

Founded in 2000, White Water Rafting Co. started as a small family business with a passion for adventure. Over the years, we have grown into one of the most trusted rafting companies in the region, offering thrilling experiences to thousands of customers annually. Our expert guides, top-notch equipment, and commitment to safety ensure that every trip is unforgettable.

From our humble beginnings on the local rivers, we've expanded to include multiple locations across the state, providing a variety of routes for all skill levels. Whether you're a seasoned rafter or a first-timer, White Water Rafting Co. has the perfect adventure waiting for you.
